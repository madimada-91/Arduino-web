⚡ Neural Architecture

How Bluetooth commands become motion: intelligence behind the machine

🔹 Bluetooth Control Loop

HC-05 module receives ASCII commands from a smartphone or web dashboard. Arduino decodes 'F','B','L','R','S' instructions and maps them to motor shield logic. Real-time direction and speed are updated every 30ms, enabling smooth steering with differential drive.

🔹 Motor Driver Intelligence

L293D acts as a current amplifier: it takes low-current logic signals from the UNO and drives the 4 gear motors. PWM signals modulate velocity for precise turns and acceleration control. Each motor pair operates independently, allowing tank-style rotation.

🔹 Power & Endurance

Dual 3.7V 2000mAh Li-ion cells in series deliver stable 7.4V to both the motor shield and Arduino. The power distribution ensures peak torque while maintaining logic voltage regulation, granting over 45 minutes of aggressive runtime.
